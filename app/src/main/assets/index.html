How to Play
🎯 The Goal
🔴🔴🔴🔴
Connect 4 of your discs in a row
Be the first player to get 4 discs in a line — horizontally, vertically, or diagonally. If the board fills up with no winner, it's a draw!
🕹️ How to Drop
1
Tap a column button (numbered 1–7) below the board to drop your disc into that column.
2
Discs fall to the lowest empty slot in that column. You can't pick a row directly.
3
Players alternate turns. A grayed-out column button means that column is full.
⚡ 2× Power Move
⚡
Spend 🪙10 coins to activate 2× Power. On your next turn you drop two discs before the opponent gets to go — a massive tactical advantage!
💡 Best used when you need one more disc to win, or to block two threats at once.
🪙 Coins & Rewards
🏆Win a level → earn +5 coins
📺Watch an ad → earn +10 coins
✨Watch ad after win → ×3 bonus coins
⚡Use 2× Power → costs −10 coins
📊 Levels & Difficulty
★ EASYLevels 1–300 · Bot makes mistakes often
★★ NORMALLevels 301–600
★★★ HARDLevels 601–900
★★★★ EXPERTLevels 901–1200
★★★★★ MASTERLevels 1201–1500 · Near-perfect bot
💡 Strategy Tips
→Control the center column (Col 4). More winning lines pass through the center than any edge.
→Always block your opponent if they have 3 in a row — never let them complete 4.
→Build two threats at once (a "double threat"). Your opponent can only block one!
→Avoid placing discs that help your opponent build above yours in the same column.
